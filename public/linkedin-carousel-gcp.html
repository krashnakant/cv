How to Export as PDF for LinkedIn

  1. Press Cmd+P (Mac) or Ctrl+P (Windows)
  2. Select "Save as PDF" as destination
  3. Set paper size to Custom: 540 x 675 px or use "Fit to page"
  4. Save and upload to LinkedIn as a document post
